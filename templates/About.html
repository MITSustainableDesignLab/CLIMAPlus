Alpha Yacob Arsano


Alpha is an architect with a focus on building science and sustainable design. She is currently conducting her doctoral research at the MIT Sustainable Design Lab. She has been developing an early-design approach to promote the design of naturally ventilated buildings, that has been implemented in this web-based platform. The goal is to make easily accessible natural ventilation guidelines available for people who want to make their buildings energy-efficient, and low-carbon.

Christoph Reinhart


Christoph Reinhart is a building scientist and architectural educator working in the field of sustainable building design and environmental modeling. At MIT he is leading the Sustainable Design Lab, an inter-disciplinary group with a grounding in architecture that develops design workflows, planning tools and metrics to evaluate the environmental performance of buildings and neighborhoods.
